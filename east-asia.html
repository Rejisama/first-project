Awesome Places in East Asia

  • Kiyomizu-dera Temple
    tewiha_orenoyome/ Pixabay/ CC0 Public Domain

    Kiyomizu-dera is a Buddhist temple in Kyoto, Japan. It was among the 20 finalists for the New7Wonders of the World. Its main hall has a large veranda with tall pillars. Beneath it is the Otowa waterfall and pond. The temple complex also includes several shrines.

  • Naejangsan National Park
    sharonang/ Pixabay/ CC0 Public Domain

    Naejangsan National Park was designated as the 8th national park of South Korea. It is home to a thousand plant and animal species. It is a popular destination for people who want to see beautiful red trees during the autumn season.

  • Tian Tan Buddha
    Béria L. Rodríguez/ Wikipedia/ CC BY-SA 3.0

    Tian Tan Buddha is a massive bronze statue of Buddha in Lantau Island, Hong Kong. It symbolizes the harmonious relationship between nature, man and faith. It is a major center of Buddhism and tourism in Hong Kong.

  • Kinkaku-ji
    wake99/ Pixabay/ CC0 Public Domain

    Kinkaku-ji is a three-story Buddhist temple in Kyoto, Japan. It means "Temple of the Golden Pavilion" in Japanese for its gold-like coating. This World Heritage Site is one of the country's most popular tourist attractions.
